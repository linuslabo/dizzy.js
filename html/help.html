A NEW WAY OF PRESENTING

Throw your ideas and images onto an open canvas and move, zoom, rotate them around! Let your audience enjoy, understand and remember your presentations much more!





Dizzy is totally free and let you:
  • EDIT cool vectorial plane-embedded presentation
  • EXPORT them as a small .svg file (with average size of less than 1 MB)
  • Simply PLAY them in your browser (even offline) without the need of additional plugins
Unlike similar famous applications, Dizzy is totally:
  • OS-FREE =D
Just needs a modern browser to work, Mozilla Firefox (9+) is highly recommended.
